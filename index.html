Privacy Policy

Wrong Drive Racing Car
Developer: Ar Technology BD
Platform: Android
Effective: March 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wrong Drive Racing Car, developed by Ar Technology BD ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you use our Android mobile game. By playing the game, you agree to the practices described below.

📱

Information We Collect

01

Wrong Drive Racing Car is a locally-operated game. We do not directly collect or store personal data on our own servers. The following data is managed within the App:

  • Local game data: Your best score, total coins, music preference, and equipped item states are saved via Android SharedPreferences on your device only.
  • No registration required: The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • No user-generated content: The App does not allow uploading, posting, or sharing any content.
✅

We never collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.

📢

Advertising – Google AdMob

02

Wrong Drive Racing Car uses Google AdMob to serve rewarded video advertisements (for jumps, lifelines, and shop coins). AdMob may automatically collect certain data to deliver and personalise ads, which may include:

  • Device identifiers – Advertising ID, Android ID
  • Network information – IP address, approximate location (country/region level)
  • Device details – Device type, OS version, language
  • Ad interaction data – Impressions, clicks, reward completions
  • App diagnostics – Usage and performance data
ℹ️

Data collected by AdMob is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Review it at policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out of personalised ads via Device Settings → Google → Ads → Delete Advertising ID.

🇪🇺

GDPR Consent – European Users

03

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the App presents a consent form via Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) before displaying ads. This form allows you to:

  • Accept or decline personalised advertising
  • Understand which ad partners may receive your data
  • Withdraw or update your consent at any time

If personalised advertising consent is declined, only non-personalised ads will be shown.

⚙️

How We Use Information

04

Locally stored game data is used solely to:

  • Save your progress, best score, and coin balance across sessions
  • Remember in-game preferences such as music and equipped items
  • Provide the rewarded ad experience (jumps, lifelines, shop coins)
🚫

We do not use any information for profiling, marketing communications, or sale to third parties.

🔗

Data Sharing

05

We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal data. Data may be shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • Google AdMob: Ad-serving data handled by Google under their own privacy policy (see Section 2).
  • Legal obligation: Disclosure may occur if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or crash-reporting services are integrated in the current version of this App.

🪙

In-App Coins & Virtual Items

06
⚠️

The coin and shop system in Wrong Drive Racing Car is entirely virtual. Coins are earned by playing or watching rewarded ads. There are no real-money purchases in this App. We never collect payment or financial information.

👶

Children's Privacy

07

Wrong Drive Racing Car is a general-audience game and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through our App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information. Google AdMob can serve family-safe, non-personalised ads where required by applicable law (e.g. COPPA).

🔒

Data Storage & Security

08

All game state (scores, coins, preferences) is stored locally on your Android device using Android's SharedPreferences mechanism. This data:

  • Never leaves your device through our systems
  • Is protected by your device's standard Android security model
  • Will be deleted if you uninstall the App or clear App data via Android Settings

We implement commercially reasonable measures to protect the App, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

⚖️

Your Rights

09

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access or request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict data processing
  • Withdraw advertising consent at any time (see Section 3)
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
ℹ️

Since we store no personal data on our own servers, most rights can be exercised directly on your device (clear App data via Android Settings) or through Google's privacy controls for AdMob data.
